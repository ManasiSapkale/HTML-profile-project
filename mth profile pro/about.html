About us

MC Talent Hunt is a Technical Training and IT Placement Service Provider. Our firm was established with the vision of bridging the gap between the Job Seekers and the Industrial Recruitments in India. Currently, we specifically cater to the needs of Information Technology firms across the country. As a proud partner of several IT giants, MC Talent Hunt has successfully placed the deserving candidates in the top-notch firms with a satisfaction rate of 100%.
